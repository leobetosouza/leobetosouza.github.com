About Me

Mar 3rd, 2014

Hello! My fullname is Leonardo Alberto Augusto de Jesus Souza (yep, too long!), but I prefer simply Leobetosouza (easier, right?).

I’m a Web Developer who was born and live in Rio de Janeiro/RJ (yeah! It’s really a marvelous city)

Currently I work for Estante Virtual and I study to be a B.S. in Information Systems at Fluminense Federal University (UFF), in Niterói/RJ (that is the university campus that have the best view of the marvelousness of Rio).

My primary fields of academic interest are Web Development, Semantics and Accessibility and my favorite programming languages are JavaScript and Perl.

If you want to know more about my professional life you can go to my LinkedIn and GitHub profiles.

For more personal infos follow me on Twitter or Facebook (knowing that I only accept friend requests from people I actually know ;))

Here in the blog you will find some of my thoughts about web development and related things, usually in Portuguese (I’m sorry. I’ll write in English sometimes. I swear!).

So long and thanks for the visit!
